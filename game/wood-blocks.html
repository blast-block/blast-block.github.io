About Wood Blocks

Wood Blocks is the fun puzzle strategy game in which you must place different shaped wooden blocks into the play area, similar to 10x10. You will place the blocks one by one into the play area and they will drop onto one another using gravity.

The goal of this game is to create a complete line and make the blocks disappear. This gives you points towards your score. There are three different shaped blocks to use. Plan your moves carefully as you will quickly become stuck with gaps between your blocks that you cannot fill. Good luck and place those blocks!

How to Play

In Wood Blocks, you are given three different shaped blocks to choose from. Drag and drop the shapes onto the game board, where they will fall due to gravity. Your goal is to create complete horizontal or vertical lines to make the blocks disappear and earn points.

Strategic thinking is crucial in this game. You need to plan ahead and consider where each block will land. As the game progresses, free space becomes limited, making it increasingly challenging to place new blocks without creating gaps that can't be filled later.

Features

  • Tetris on a board gameplay
  • 3 shapes in each turn
  • 2 background color options

Tips and Strategies

  • Always think ahead and consider your next few moves
  • Try to keep the board as flat as possible
  • Focus on completing lines to clear space
  • Be careful not to create small gaps that can't be filled
  • Use the different shapes strategically to maximize your points

Controls

Drag the left mouse button to place a block to the field.

On mobile devices, simply tap and drag with your finger to place blocks.
