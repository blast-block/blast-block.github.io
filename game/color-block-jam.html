About Color Block Jam

Color Block Jam is a puzzle game where players need to slide colored blocks into corresponding colored doors while avoiding obstacles. Challenge yourself to reach higher levels!

How to Play

Avoid obstacles - Slide the colored blocks into the correct colored doors while avoiding various obstacles that could block your path.

Think ahead! Color Block Jam requires strategic thinking and quick reflexes to succeed in the later levels. The game gets progressively more challenging, so plan your moves wisely.
