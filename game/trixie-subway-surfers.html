About Trixie Subway Surfers

Trixie Subway Surfers is an exciting new take on the classic endless runner game. You play as Trixie, a new character with magical abilities who surfs the subways and tries to escape from the grumpy Inspector and his dog. You'll need to dodge trains, trams, obstacles, and more to go as far as you can in this endless running game.

Collect coins to unlock power-ups and special gear to help you go further every time in Trixie Subway Surfers. Furthermore, coins can be used to unlock different characters and boards. With your keys you can customize the characters and upgrade your hoverboards with special powers. Don't forget to complete the awards, since they give you keys. In 'MyTour' you can collect rewards from completing daily Word Hunts. You can also find missions there.

Trixie Subway Surfers was created by Sybo in 2024 as a new version of the highly popular Subway Surfers game. The original Subway Surfers reached a record 1.8 billion downloads according to Sybo Games (2021), and this new version continues the exciting legacy with fresh characters and enhanced gameplay.

Now in HTML5, you can play the game on your mobile phone and tablet online in your browser exclusively here. Next to that, you can still enjoy playing Trixie Subway Surfers on your PC. You can play the game for free without downloading it.

Subway Surfers Characters

Subway Surfers features a diverse cast of colorful characters, each with their own unique abilities and personality. Check out our complete character guide for detailed information on each character's abilities, special powers, and how to unlock them!

  • Trixie: The newest subway surfer with special magical abilities! Her enchanted skateboard lets her perform exceptional tricks and maneuvers.
  • Jake: The original subway surfer and our main character! Jake has balanced abilities and is perfect for beginners.
  • Tricky: A real perfectionist and the brains of the Surfers! Her special coin magnet ability helps collect more coins.
  • Yutani: A tech genius and science obsessed. She also thinks she's not from this world and has antigravity abilities!
  • Lucy: Our resident punk in the streets, someone has to give the guard a hard time. Specializes in quick reflexes and sharp turns.
  • Tagbot: The first robot in Subway Surfers! Has enhanced scanning abilities to detect obstacles from further away.

Controls

You play this game using your keyboard:

  • Left/right arrow - Move left/right
  • Up arrow - Jump
  • Down arrow - Roll
  • Space - Activate hoverboard

On mobile, use swipe gestures: swipe left/right to move, swipe up to jump, swipe down to roll, and double-tap to activate hoverboard.

Character-Specific Controls

Different characters may have unique abilities that can be activated in specific ways:

  • Trixie's Magic: Double tap space bar to activate Trixie's magical shield (PC)
  • Jake's Sprint: Hold shift while running to activate temporary speed boost
  • Tricky's Magnet: Automatically activates when collecting special power-ups
