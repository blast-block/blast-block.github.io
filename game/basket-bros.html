About BasketBros

BasketBros is a fast-paced basketball game with online and offline gameplay. Choose a basketballer, customize your style, and compete in singleplayer and multiplayer games. Throw down some sick dunks and win the game to earn upgrades and unlockables.

How to Play BasketBros

Get ready for an exciting basketball experience with multiple gameplay modes:

Customize Your Player

Select your character and personalize your look. Unlock new characters and clothing as you play more games, creating your unique basketball persona.

Game Modes

Choose between quick games, tournaments, online multiplayer, or local two-player mode. Fight for the ball and outscore your opponents to win!

Practice Makes Perfect

Use the practice mode to hone your skills before jumping into competitive matches. Learn the controls and develop your strategy.

FAQ

How do I host a multiplayer game?

Click "host" in the main menu, choose your player, and copy the invite code to send to your friend.

How do I block in BasketBros?

Press the spacebar when near your opponent to execute a block.

What platforms can I play on?

BasketBros is playable on web browsers for desktop and mobile devices.
